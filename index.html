Our story

Established in 2020, Napper Engineering started off as a family run business designing and manufacting bespoke steel fabrications. We very quickly became a reputable business and it didn't take long before we needed to expand. Since production began we have moved to a larger premises, heavily invested in our equipment and quadrupled our ever-growing workforce.
